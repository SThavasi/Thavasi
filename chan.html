About Us

We are a prestigious college providing top-notch education to our students. Our commitment to excellence has made us a leader in education. Rajah Serfoji Government College is a government college in Thanjavur, Tamilnadu, India. This college is affiliated to Bharathidasan University, Thiruchiraplli. It is located in the heart of the Thanjavur city.
